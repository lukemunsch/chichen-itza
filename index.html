Yucatán, Mexico

These ruins are built in the Eastern section of Yucatán state of Mexico. The size of the site spans across 8 square kilometres, much of which has been excavated. There are twelve main highlights at the ruins, including the Temple of the Feathered Serpent Kukulkan (El Castillo), the hall of the thousand columns, the temple of the warriors and the great Ballcourt. Settlers would have first arrived around 250-500ad and would have been home to nearly 35 000 people
